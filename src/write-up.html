About

What country has the best vacation weather? Have temperatures around the world risen? Should we be panicking? I'm not panicking. Are you panicking? This visualization is made to answer these questions. The interactive chart and chloropleth of global yearly mean climate data from land observations between 1750 and 2013 (Berkeley climate data) provides a detailed and interactive way to visualize climate data.
